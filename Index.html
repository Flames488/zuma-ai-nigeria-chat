Setup

Up and running in minutes

No technical skills required. Just your WhatsApp number and business details.

Create your account & connect WhatsApp

2
Set up your business profile

Add your business name, products, prices, open hours, and your preferred tone (formal, friendly, or casual). Zuma AI uses this to craft every reply.

Go live — Zuma handles the rest

Your AI assistant starts responding to customers instantly. Monitor everything from your dashboard and step in whenever you need to.
